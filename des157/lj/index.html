By Salim Ansari

The main takeaway I grasped from the tips shared in the article is the benefits of simple and straightforward visual cues. It is important to remember that oftentimes, less is more. Simplicity is favored over complex layout in form design that hopes to provide a good user experience. A straightforward visual layout such as the one column layout makes the process of filling out a form faster and easier. The spacing of a layout is equally important. Appropriate sizing of each field of question makes it less confusing for the user. For instance, the field sizing of the width asking for the zip code should be smaller than the address line. The article emphasizes the importance of omitting unnecessary questions and to get straight to the point. Form design that is visually clean in layout and hierarchy produces better user experiences.
